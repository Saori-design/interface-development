Delicious Banana Bread

Active Time 40 min

Total Time 60 min

Calories 900 cal

Skill level Easy

Ingredients

  • Flour 100g
  • Overripe bananas 2
  • Brown sugar 100g
  • Large eggs 3
  • Baking soda tsp 1
  • Butter 100g
  • Vanilla extract half tsp

Equipment

  • Bowls
  • Whisk
  • Oven
  • Measure

Smashing

1. Smash the overripe bananas.

Mixing

2. Mix all ingredients, and pit it into the mould.

Baking

3. Put in 180c preheated oven, and bake 35 minutes with 200c.

Complete

4. Enjoy with butter, whipped cream, or honey.
